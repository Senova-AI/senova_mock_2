Senova AI is committed to your privacy and security.
We do not steal, sell, or misuse your data.

Our extension only observes the questions you ask to selected Large Language Models (LLMs) such as Gemini, Claude, and ChatGPT. We never access your account details, passwords, or any private information.

Based on your chat prompts, we generate quizzes and games to help you learn and engage better.
No chats are sent, we just send analysis of games to our server, not linked to your prompts or chats.

This is a beta version. For the best experience, please use Senova AI on Google Chrome and with Gemini.

Note: This extension currently works only on PC for GPT LLMs. Mobile support is under development.

  • You must give consent before any chat is analyzed.
  • You can revoke your consent at any time in the extension settings.
  • We do not store or share your chat history.
  • By using this extension, you agree to these terms.
